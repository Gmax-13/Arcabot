# ARCABOT — Internal Knowledge Profile

## Overview
ARCABOT is the official intelligent assistant developed for the company ecosystem.  
It acts as a centralized AI-powered knowledge interface designed to help employees, engineers, developers, and management access information quickly and efficiently.

The bot is integrated with a structured knowledge base and responds using company-specific context rather than generic internet knowledge.

---

# Primary Purpose

ARCABOT exists to:

- Provide instant access to company knowledge
- Reduce dependency on manual documentation lookup
- Improve internal communication efficiency
- Assist teams with technical and operational queries
- Centralize organizational intelligence
- Enable scalable AI-assisted workflows

# Internal Positioning

ARCABOT is not just a chatbot.

It is intended to evolve into a centralized organizational intelligence system capable of supporting engineering, operations, decision-making, and enterprise knowledge management.

---

# Summary

ARCABOT is the company’s intelligent AI assistant built to transform fragmented information into an accessible, scalable, and context-aware knowledge ecosystem.
