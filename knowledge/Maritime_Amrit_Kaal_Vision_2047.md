# MARITIME AMRIT KAAL VISION 2047
## India’s Strategic Maritime Transformation Roadmap

---

# Overview

Maritime Amrit Kaal Vision 2047 (MAKV 2047) is the long-term strategic roadmap developed by the Ministry of Ports, Shipping and Waterways (MoPSW), Government of India.

The vision defines India’s maritime transformation strategy for becoming:
- A global maritime leader
- A major blue economy power
- A sustainable maritime ecosystem
- A technologically advanced maritime nation
- A world-class logistics and shipping hub

The roadmap aligns with:
- Viksit Bharat 2047
- Maritime India Vision 2030
- Sagarmala Programme
- PM Gati Shakti
- Blue Economy framework
- Atmanirbhar Bharat
- National Logistics Policy

---

# Official Objective

MAKV 2047 aims to create:
- A globally competitive maritime ecosystem
- High-capacity smart ports
- Sustainable maritime infrastructure
- Indigenous shipbuilding capabilities
- Advanced maritime logistics systems
- Technology-driven maritime operations

India possesses:

# Final Summary

Maritime Amrit Kaal Vision 2047 is India’s comprehensive maritime transformation roadmap designed to establish the country as a technologically advanced, sustainable, globally competitive, and strategically dominant maritime power by the year 2047 through infrastructure modernization, digital transformation, smart ports, green shipping, blue economy growth, and indigenous maritime capability development.
